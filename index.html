My name is Nebiyu Waliyi Tekesa.

A PhD Researcher: Sustainable development and Climate Change (PhD-SDC

Nebiyu is currently pursuing a PhD in Sustainable Development and Climate Change at IUSS Pavia and the University of Pavia, with a research focus on extreme rainfall events in the context of climate change, particularly across Central and Northern Italy. He holds an MSc in Climate Change and a BSc in Meteorology and Hydrology. Before his doctoral studies, Nebiyu served as a researcher and lecturer at Arba Minch University in Ethiopia from August 2021 until commencing his study leave (January 2025). Nebiyu is a member of the international climate science initiative Fresh Eyes on CMIP (Coupled Model Intercomparison Project), a scientific project organized by the World Climate Research Programme (WCRP). His research interests include: - Extreme Precipitation events and their hydrological risk - Climate Modelling - Climate change impact assessment - Adaptation and mitigation strategies.
