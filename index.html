Ciao All, My name is Nebiyu Waliyi Tekesa

A PhD Researcher: Sustainable development and Climate Change (PhD-SDC)

Nebiyu is a PhD researcher at the University of Pavia, Italy (Jan 2025–present). He is enrolled in the multidisciplinary PhD Program in Sustainable Development and Climate Change (PhD‑SDC), a national doctoral initiative delivered across more than 50 Italian universities and coordinated by the University School for Advanced Studies Pavia (IUSS Pavia). His research focuses on extreme rainfall under climate change—examining trends, physical drivers, and hydrologic/hydraulic impacts across Northern Italy. He holds an MSc in Climate Change and a BSc in Meteorology and Hydrology. Before beginning his doctoral studies, Nebiyu worked as a researcher and lecturer at Arba Minch University in Ethiopia (Aug 2021–Jan 2025). He is also a member of the international climate science initiative Fresh Eyes on CMIP, a scientific project organized by the World Climate Research Programme (WCRP). His research interests include:

  • Extreme precipitation and hydrological risk
  • Climate modelling
  • Climate change impact assessment
  • Adaptation and mitigation strategies
