My name is Nebiyu Waliyi Tekesa.

A PhD Researcher: Sustainable development and Climate Change (PhD-SDC

Nebiyu is currently (Jan 2025-on) a PhD researcher in university of Pavia, Italy. He is admitted to a Multi-disciplinary PhD in Sustainable Development and Climate Change (PhD-SDC-phd:phd-sdc.it/) which is given across more than 50 universities of Italy, coordinated by the University School for Advanced Studies Pavia (IUSSPavia). His research project focuses on extreme rainfall under climate change (Trend, drivers, and hydrologic/hydraulic effects) in Northern Italy. He holds an MSc in Climate Change and a BSc in Meteorology and Hydrology. Before his doctoral studies, Nebiyu served as a researcher and lecturer at Arba Minch University in Ethiopia from August 2021 until commencing his Study leave (January 2025). Nebiyu is a member of the international climate science initiative Fresh Eyes on CMIP (Coupled Model Intercomparison Project), a scientific project organized by the World Climate Research Programme (WCRP). His research interests include: - Extreme Precipitation events and their hydrological risk - Climate Modelling - Climate change impact assessment - Adaptation and mitigation strategies.
